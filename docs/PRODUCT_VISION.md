# Product Vision

## Product Name

OmniCore AI

## Product Category

AI-powered Commerce & Operations Intelligence Platform

## Vision Statement

OmniCore AI helps retailers run commerce operations from a single intelligent operating layer. The platform brings products, inventory, orders, fulfillment, analytics, forecasting, and decision intelligence into one maintainable system so teams can see what is happening, understand what is likely to happen next, and act with confidence.

The product should reduce operational guesswork. It should help teams answer questions like:

- Which products are at risk of stockout?
- Which orders need attention today?
- Which warehouse tasks are blocked?
- What inventory should be replenished soon?
- What trends are driving sales, returns, or fulfillment delays?
- What action should an operator take next?

## Target Users

### Customer

Customers need a simple view of their own shopping and order activity. In the MVP, customer functionality should remain focused and practical: product visibility where needed, order status, and order history.

### Inventory Manager

Inventory Managers own catalog accuracy, stock visibility, replenishment decisions, inventory adjustments, and demand planning inputs. They need fast scanning, reliable counts, low-stock alerts, and forecasting support.

### Warehouse Manager

Warehouse Managers own the operational flow from order readiness through picking, packing, shipping, and fulfillment exceptions. They need queues, status clarity, and tools to resolve blockers quickly.

### Admin

Admins own platform configuration, users, roles, permissions, and overall operational visibility. They need trustworthy controls without excessive complexity.

## Core Problem

Retail teams often manage commerce operations across fragmented tools, spreadsheets, manual reports, and disconnected AI experiments. This creates delayed decisions, duplicated work, inaccurate inventory, reactive fulfillment, and poor visibility into what is likely to happen next.

OmniCore AI exists to consolidate operational workflows and make decision support available directly inside the system where work happens.

## Product Principles

### MVP First

The first version should solve the essential daily workflows before expanding into advanced automation, integrations, or enterprise customization.

### Operational Trust

Users must trust the numbers, statuses, and recommendations they see. Data accuracy and clear provenance matter more than flashy AI output.

### Human-Controlled Intelligence

AI should recommend, explain, summarize, forecast, and prioritize. It should not silently perform irreversible operational actions.

### Simple Architecture

The platform should be easy to reason about. Use a modular monolith, separate frontend and backend, clear domain boundaries, and explicit API contracts.

### Role-Focused UX

Each role should see the workflows and insights most relevant to their work. The product should reduce noise, not add another dashboard layer.

## MVP Value Proposition

For retailers that need better control over commerce operations, OmniCore AI provides a unified operations platform that combines transactional workflows with AI-assisted forecasting and decision intelligence.

Unlike fragmented tools or isolated analytics dashboards, OmniCore AI connects day-to-day actions with operational context, historical data, and intelligent recommendations.

## MVP Product Capabilities

The MVP should include:

- Product catalog management
- Inventory visibility and adjustments
- Order management and status tracking
- Fulfillment queue management
- Role-based dashboards
- Basic operational analytics
- Demand forecasting using historical data
- RAG-based operational question answering
- AI recommendations for replenishment and operational risks
- Admin user and role management

## What The MVP Is Not

The MVP is not:

- A marketplace platform
- A full ERP replacement
- A warehouse robotics system
- A complex multi-tenant enterprise suite
- A microservices platform
- A fully autonomous AI operations agent
- A customer marketing automation platform
- A complete accounting or payments system

## Long-Term Direction

After the MVP proves the core workflows, OmniCore AI can expand toward:

- Advanced replenishment optimization
- Supplier and purchase order workflows
- Returns and reverse logistics
- Multi-warehouse inventory balancing
- Native integrations with ecommerce platforms and carriers
- Advanced anomaly detection
- Scenario planning
- Tenant-level customization
- More sophisticated AI copilots for operations teams

## Success Measures

MVP success should be measured by practical operational outcomes:

- Reduced time to identify low-stock and stockout risk
- Faster fulfillment exception resolution
- Improved visibility into order and inventory status
- Higher confidence in replenishment planning
- Reduced manual reporting work
- Clear adoption by Inventory Managers and Warehouse Managers
- Reliable admin control over users and permissions
